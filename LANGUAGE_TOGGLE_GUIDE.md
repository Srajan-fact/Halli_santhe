---

## 🔄 How to Use

### Step 1: Open the App
Launch Halli Santhe Digital and log in as a Customer or Vendor.

### Step 2: Locate the Globe Icon
Look at the **top right** of the app bar. You'll see a **globe icon (🌍)** next to the Logout button.

### Step 3: Click to Toggle
- **English → Kannada**: Click once
- **Kannada → English**: Click once again

### Step 4: Watch the Magic ✨
The entire app interface transforms instantly:
- All text changes language
- Buttons and labels update
- Your choice is automatically saved

You don't need to select the language every time:

- ✅ When you close and re-open, it stays in the same language
- ✅ Preference is stored on your device

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Where is the language button?**
A: Look at the top-right corner of the screen (next to Logout button).

**Q: Can I switch language mid-session?**
A: Yes! Switch anytime without losing your progress.

**Q: Will my choice be remembered?**
A: Yes, 100%. Close the app and it will be in the same language when you open it again.

**Q: Are authentication screens in Kannada too?**
A: Currently, the login screens use your device's default language. Main app screens fully support English ↔ Kannada toggle.

**Q: Can I add more languages?**
A: Yes, developers can easily add more languages by extending the LanguageManager (see LANGUAGE_SWITCHING_FEATURE.md).
